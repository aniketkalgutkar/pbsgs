Paschim Bharat Sanadhya Gaud Sabha was founded in March 1980

Our goal is to unite all the Gaud in Families living in Mumbai as well as in India as a whole, to eradicate evil practices of the society like dowry, to develop the society and educate people about our culture, to encourage our youth, and to help everyone affected by natural disasters and poverty.

Areas Under Operation

Karya Kshetras include Western India, that is, Maharashtra, Gujrat, Rajhasthan, Hariyana, Western Uttar Pradesh, Delhi and Madhya Pradesh.

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to bring all the Gaud and Sanadhya Brahmins staying in various places in the Country together, under one Umbrella. The goal will be to meet at one platform, know each other and help each other in all the possible ways.
The main goal will be to ensure the well-being of all the members. Our tradition and culture to be maintained and continued further. The youngsters will be involved so as they understand the essence and take it further to the next generation.
Our Sabha will be actively involved in encouraging and assisting all the needy students. Scholarships will be provided to Meritorious Students. The goal will be to raise the living standards of the economically weak families.
To make efforts to assist finding of suitable bride and bride-groom for the eligible bachelors through the Matrimonial Wing
